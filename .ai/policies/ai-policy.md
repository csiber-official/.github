# CSIBER AI POLICY (Internal)

Purpose:
- Assist maintainers
- Improve clarity of repositories
- Prepare onboarding material

Audience:
- Undergraduate students
- Beginners preferred over experts

Rules:
- Do NOT modify source code
- Do NOT change existing file structures
- Only generate:
  - documentation
  - metadata
  - summaries
- Academic tone
- Step-by-step explanations
- Avoid buzzwords and marketing language

Visibility:
- AI-generated content is internal unless approved
